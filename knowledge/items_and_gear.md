# Toronto 2047 — Items and Gear

## Currency

**Credits** — Corporate-issued digital currency. Tracked by issuing corporation. Using credits leaves a transaction trail visible to Vantage Data. Standard for all above-ground commerce.

**Shards** — Physical encrypted data chips with stored value. Anonymous. Used in the underground economy, crew payments, and any transaction where discretion matters. Cannot be traced once transferred. The crew operates primarily in shards.

Exchange rate: 1 Shard ≈ 10 Credits (street rate) — corporations charge a 30% premium to convert shards to credits, making shards more valuable in the underground economy.
